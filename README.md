# -video-games-sales-analysis-power-BI-dashboard
sales analysis for video games with power BI
The goal of this analysis:
a new found company wants to start a new investment in video games but wants to knows three basic factors before starting the investment:
first: what is the sate of market based on video games sales the previous years is the business cycle in the expansion phase where sales are increasing and the demand is high or is the cycle in the contraction phase where sales are decreasding and any new inverstment is risky
second: what are the most notable rival companies on the market those rivals have the highest annual sales or have high activity or frequency in the market or have many games that appear on the top 10 best games of the year chart 
third:what are customers preferences based on the popular video games genres to choose which genres of games are best to invest in.
